NFTsurance

New Standard in Protection

Problem

With the Cambrian explosion of NTF mints on Solana, scams become a reoccuring phenomenon that only seems to increase in frequency. Malicious actors abuse unreliable KYC methods, make preposterous promises or straight up try to drain the wallets of minters. Especially the Solana NFT space has become a lawless zone like never before. As it is difficult to uncover scams before they are happening,
even experienced NFT traders still fall for them from time to time, and sadly the mentality has shifted to a point where this is accepted as a tolerable risk. That is where NFTsurance comes in to play, by becoming a part of the NFTsurance DAO, members no longer need to fear a mint as they get compensatet for every SOL they loseso they can ape freely like never before.

Concept

While trying to reimburse everyone for their losses sounds like a great idea, how will this actually be implemented? Working together with our community we will publish a weekly Safelist with all the mints covered by NFTsurance. Holders of the NFTsurance then will in turn be able to earn insurancepoints (IP), that will be exchanged 1:1 for SOL in the case that any of the projects on the Safelist and they invested in rugs. IP will be earned accordingly to the amount and rarity of the NFTs staked (weekly earnings):
1 NFT: 5+ NFT: 10+ NFT:
Common: 0.1 IP 0.6 IP 1.5 IP
Rare: 0.3 IP 1.8 IP 4.5 IP
Legendary: 0.5 IP 3 IP 7.5 IP

Financing

Just like any real world insurance company we also need to generate a steady inflow of cash in order to make our project longterm sustainable. Unlike other insurance companies we dont take a monthly fee for our insurance service,
but instead a one time payment of the mint price. So how are we able to pay our community members if a rug occurs? We are not afraid to give back a large portion of the collected funds and will try to increase the reserves with:
  • Staking
    +
    Farming
  • Royalties
  • IaaS
  • Securing Mints

Security

The devaluation of NFTs post mint is alomst as severe as an actual rug, oftentimes the value of NFTs is halfed after a couple of days, with almost no buyers remaining to even bail you out at these low prices. We do not expect this project to ever fall under mint price, but we want to inspire additional confidence in our investors. What many people tend to forget is that the money is not gone after the mint,
it is now just in the hands of the team, and most of the time they are not willing to give it back, We do. Right after the successful mint we will launch a redemption-contract that will enable anyone who owns one of our NFTs to redeem it for its initial cost minus a small fee, this guarantees for the price to stay afloat.

Roadmap

Mint

The initial mint of the first 5000 NFTsurances, concluded on our own minting page, with the help of experienced developers

Redemption Contract

Shortly after mint we will launch the aforementioned redemption-contract, that ensures a stable floor price and prevents any potential moneygrab

Safelists

ASAP we will publish the first weekly Safelist for all the projects you can mint without having to worry about a rug

IP Token

In order to automate the whole insurance process we will deploy a staking contract that issues non tradeable and non transferable InsurancePoints to track your eligibility for recompensation

Partnerships

In order to maintain the occuring costs we will check up on other mints and give them credebility for their launch in return for a precentage of their tokens

Governance

Transition from a manual selection and voting process of safelisting projects to an classic on-chain DAO, that governs over the whole procedure and future developments

New Standard

Work on implementing our redemption process as a standard in the whole Solana ecosystem for all credible projects

FAQs

Where can I purchase a NFTsurance?

The initial 5000 NFTsurances will be minted on our website, for more infos follow us on Twitter or join the Discord. Afterwards will have listings on all major marketplaces.

How does NFTsurane protect me?

We will use the majority of community funds and all our income streams to regularly cover all SOL lost due to malicious mints. For details you can read the above sections on Implementation and Financing.

Which projects are covered by NFTsurance?

Of course we can't cover every single mint on Solana, therefore we let our community decide what projects to cover and publish a weekly Safelist with all the protected mints.

How do the IP work?

Insurance Points keep track of the size of covarage for each holder, the more and the rarer NFTs you hold the more of your mints are protected.
